Detroit Community Table

This is the final project my group produced for our JavaScript bootcamp at Grand Circus Detroit. Everyday at lunch we would trade restaurant recommendations, which we then, of course, forgot by the next day. We decided to build a Detroit specific app that allowed a user to save recomendations and search local restaurants.

This Site

This site was built with Gulp taskrunners, Sass for a preprocessor, and Bourbon's Neat grid for an easily maintainable grid that lives in the CSS. It lives in a single-page AngularJS app and relies on jQuery for its user-generated actions.
